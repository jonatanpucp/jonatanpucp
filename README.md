## About me

I am an economist from the Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú
interested in applied microeconomics, causal inference, and data-driven
research.

My work focuses on development economics, economics of education,
political economy, and environmental economics. I use Python, R, and
Stata to construct and analyze large databases, implement econometric
methods, and produce reproducible research.

## Selected methods

`Causal Inference` · `Difference-in-Differences` · `Regression Discontinuity`

`Instrumental Variables` · `Panel Data` · `Spatial Econometrics`

`Machine Learning` · `Geospatial Analysis`
